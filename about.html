About us

Let Us Tell You Our Story

Selema Farms was incorporated in 2012 as a player in the Agriculture sector, we started with importing and trading in fruits and vegetables. In 2016, we took over the Oloba Ranch, which started our journey into the livestock business. Since then, we began our own breeding program, where we're cross breeding the Sokoto Red Goat with our local WAD ( West African Dwarf ) and we can see improvement in the breed. We are also crossing the Balami Rams with our WAD Sheeps to produce improve breeds. We also do fattening programn for Rams every Sallah and we do fattening for Goats and Bulls, for weight gain, that we process to supply our various customers. We have also built our own Abattoir and Cold Rooms on the farm, for ease of operation and makes us more vertical integrated.

Our Mission

To enable Nigerians Cultivate Healthy Meat Eating Habit.

Our Vision

To become one of Africa's LiveStock Production House, with an eye on exportation to Foreign Markets.

Our Objective

To enable Nigerians Cultivate Healthy Meat Eating Habit.
