About us

Let Us Tell You Our Story

Selema Farms was incorporated in 2012 as a player in the Agriculture sector, we started with importing and trading in fruits and vegetables. In 2016, we took over the Oloba Ranch, which started our journey into the livestock business.

What we do

Our Work speaks for us

Here at selema Farms Our goal is to provide Healthy Livestock farming for consumption and investment for Nigerians and through our farm practices and business model we've been able to provide

Healthy & Livestock

All our livestock are of the best of health,
we ensure that they are bred to the highest standard and in the best of conditions.

Animal Cross Breeding

We cross breed our animals to create the healthiest hybrid livestock,which in turn allows for improvement in breed.

Fattening Program

We fatten up our animals in anticipation for year round festivities so our clientele gets the most out of their purchase with us.

A source of investment

We also provide investment opportunities through our crowdfunding platform
